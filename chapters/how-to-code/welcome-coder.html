3.5. Welcome, New Coder¶

Congratulations! You are ready to take the next steps in learning to code.

Some words of advice:

  1. Your instructor and classmates are here to help.

  2. Complete all of the homework and practice tasks. To learn how to code, you need to CODE.

  3. Ask and answer questions.

  4. Coding mistakes happen a LOT, but they are part of the learning process. Don’t be afraid to experiment!

We look forward to your success.
